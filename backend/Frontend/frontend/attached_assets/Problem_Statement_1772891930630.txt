Name: Infrabox
Tagline:

 “Predict. Protect. Deploy.”
Concise System Explanation
Infrabox is an AI-powered DevOps intelligence platform that analyzes a project's codebase and CI/CD pipeline to predict infrastructure risks before deployment. When a repository is connected, Infrabox simulates the pipeline inside a sandbox environment, studies service dependencies, and models traffic and resource usage. It detects potential failures, scaling bottlenecks, and abnormal cloud cost patterns in advance.
The system then generates a deployment confidence score and suggests mitigation actions such as scaling services, optimizing pipelines, or fixing configuration issues. If the user approves, specialized AI agents automatically execute these fixes. Developers can also interact with a conversational assistant to investigate incidents, understand infrastructure behavior, and get optimization insights.
Once risks are resolved, Infrabox integrates with deployment platforms to safely deploy the application and continue monitoring infrastructure health.
In simple terms, Infrabox acts as an AI DevOps control layer that predicts problems, recommends solutions, and ensures safer deployments.
